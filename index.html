Hardware

F450 QUADCOPTER FRAME 4-AXIS

It's a 450mm quad frame made out of high-quality materials. The arms are made of ultra-durable polyamide nylon, while the main structure is made of glass fibre.

BRUSHLESS MOTOR

A2212 is a brushless out runner DC motor specifically made to power Quadcopters and Multirotor. It is a 2200kV motor

PROPELLER

A set of 2 x 1045 Propellers. The set includes one clockwise rotating and one counter-clockwise rotating propeller with 2 x propeller shaft adapters.

ECS

30A BLDC ESC ELECTRONIC SPEED CONTROLLER can drive motors which consume up to 30A current.
